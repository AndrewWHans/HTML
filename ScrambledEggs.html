Scrambling Eggs

Ordered list: How to make some simple scrambled eggs

  1. Ensure to have a pan
  2. Acquire eggs and place on the countertop
  3. Acquire butter and have a slice of butter to ensure the pan is not sticky.
  4. Heat the pan
  5. Crack the eggs into the pan
  6. Stir until the eggs aren't in liquid form
  7. Once eggs look softly set and slightly runny in places, turn off stovetop
  8. Place the eggs on a plate, enjoy!

Unordered list example:

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 100ml milk
  • 50g salted butter
  • Freshly grated nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
